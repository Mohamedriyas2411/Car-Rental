About Us

With transparent tracking and location assistance, once the car is returned, documents are verified, and a final bill is generated based on duration and distance with 5 to 10% of service charges will be added, ensuring a smooth, reliable rental experience for both parties.

"Swift Drive Car Rentals" Founded in 2023, was created to make car rentals more affordable and accessible for both car owners and renters. Our platform enables car owners to rent their vehicles and earn extra income, while offering renters a variety of vehicles to choose from, all at competitive prices.
